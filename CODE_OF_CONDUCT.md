# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and constructive project for people of every background and experience level. Participation should make the project and its community better.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, specific, and generous in technical discussion.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while still addressing harmful impact.
- Welcome questions and explain project conventions without gatekeeping.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licences, and requests to stop an interaction.
- Accept maintainer decisions gracefully, including requests to narrow or revise a contribution.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private information.
- Deliberately submitting malicious code, secrets, private data, or licence-incompatible assets.
- Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, community spaces, and private interactions related to Potaty.

## Reporting and enforcement

For an urgent or sensitive conduct report, use the repository's private advisory form at <https://github.com/potatohoney-p/potaty/security/advisories/new> and begin the title with `Code of Conduct`. For a non-sensitive moderation request, contact a maintainer through GitHub.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue a warning, restrict participation, or permanently ban an account. Reports will be handled as privately as practical, and conflicts of interest will be avoided where possible.
